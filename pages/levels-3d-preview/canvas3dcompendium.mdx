# 3D Canvas Mapmaking Pack

<ModuleInfo />
An asset pack that includes tons of 3D Tiles, Skyboxes, and Materials to help you
make your maps look amazing in 3D Canvas!

<Callout type='info' emoji='ℹ️'>
  Not Limited to 3D! You can use the textures included in this pack for 2D Map
  building directly in FVTT.
</Callout>

## How to Install:

Simply copy paste the Manifest URL into the install module window manifest URL filed inside foundry

Manifest URL:

```txt copy
https://github.com/theripper93/canvas3dcompendium/releases/latest/download/module.json
```

Forge Users: https://forge-vtt.com/bazaar/package/canvas3dcompendium

## Material Browser

    <ImageButBetter
      src="/images/3D-Canvas/Material_Browser_Icon.png"
      alt="Material Browser Button"
      className={styles.wikiimage}
    />

    If you installed the 3D Canvas Mapmaking Pack, you will have access to more than 1000 Materials and the Material Browser.
    <ImageButBetter
      src="/images/3D-Canvas/Material_Browser.png"
      alt="Material Browser Interface"
      className={styles.wikiimage}
    />

    To access the material browser simply click the circular icon left of the Filepicker icon (<FontAwesomeIcon icon={faFileImport} />).


    You will then be welcomed by the material browser. To apply the material simply click on the desired one. Keep in mind that if you intend to use these materials on tokens, you need to select PBR in the material
    option.
