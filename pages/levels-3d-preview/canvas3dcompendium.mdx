# 3D Canvas Mapmaking Pack

<ModuleInfo />
An asset pack that includes tons of 3D Tiles, Skyboxes, and Materials to help you
make your maps look amazing in 3D Canvas!

<Callout type='info' emoji='ℹ️'>
  Not Limited to 3D! You can use the textures included in this pack for 2D Map
  building directly in FVTT.
</Callout>

## How to Install:

Simply search for `3D Canvas Mapmaking Pack` in the module manager and install it.

    You will then be welcomed by the material browser. To apply the material simply click on the desired one. Keep in mind that if you intend to use these materials on tokens, you need to select PBR in the material

    If you installed the 3D Canvas Mapmaking Pack, you will have access to more than 3000 3D Tiles!

    To access the asset browser simply click the icon shown in the image (make sure to be in the 3D View!)

    You will then be welcomed by the asset browser.

    - To place a 3D Model, simply drag and drop it onto your scene.
    - You can use the search bar to filter the assets and the Scale field to resize assets before placing them.
    - Left click on an asset to select it, left click again to deselect it. Selecting an asset will set it for quick placement. You can hold CTRL to select multiple assets.
    - The asset browser also has some advanced quick placement options, check out [this short video](https://youtu.be/WgNMPGs3A60) for a quick overview

## Quick Terrain

    <Image
      src="/images/3D-Canvas/quickterrainbtn.png"
      alt="Quick Terrain Button"
      className={styles.wikiImage}
    />

    <Image
      src="/images/3D-Canvas/quickterrain.png"
      alt="Quick Terrain Interface"
      className={styles.wikiImage}
    />
The Quick Terrain feature allows you to get a quick start in your mapmaking by creating a randomize terrain with some preset themes.

- **Generate**: get a new terrain or re-randomize the current one.
- **Variation**: get a different iteration of the current terrain.
- **Fine Tune**: adjust the heightmap position and scale manually.
- **Add Water**:  create a water tile
- **Apply Theme**: apply the currently selected theme to the terrain.

Check [this short video](https://youtu.be/xRoKgdBipKU) for a quick overview.
