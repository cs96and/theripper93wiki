# DnD5e - System Customizer

<ModuleInfo/>

Customize the D&D5e system to your liking.

## How to use

Simply head to the `Configure Settings` menu, then head to the 'DnD5e - System Customizer' customization menu.

## Limitations

### Spell Levels

While it's possible to add spell levels above 9th, due to the immutable nature of some parts of the dnd5e data structure, you won't be able to add spell slots of those levels to the character sheet. That said, you will still be able to cast those spells.
