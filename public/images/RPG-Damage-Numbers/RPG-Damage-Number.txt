== How to use ==

=== With Midi-QoL ===
If you have Midi-QoL in use, RPG Damage Numbers will automatically show colored damage numbers whenever a creature is damaged.
[[File:Rdnsettings.png|thumb|Module Settings|519x519px]]

=== Without Midi-QoL ===
If you are not using Midi-QoL, RPG Damage numbers will still display it's own custom animation for status effects and damage numbers, but color will not be shown for different damage types.

Make sure that <code>Override Core Bouncing Text</code> is enabled in the module settings
